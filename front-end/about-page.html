Hello

Hello! My name is Emily and this is my living resume. I created this to showcase my work and to help you learn more about me. I started coding in my senior year of high school and applied to colleges to become a computer scientist three months later. I'd say I made the right decision.

My fascination with coding came from playing video games with my family - Dark Souls, Legend of Zelda, Horizons, Skyrim, you name it, we played it. Seeing how creative and limitless programming can be, I wondered what else I could do with coding..

That train of thought moved me up to CSU Monterey Bay to study programming. With endless opportunities to explore, my interests grew, and I am now particularly interested in Cyber Security and all the ins and outs of it.
